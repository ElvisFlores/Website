About the Course

This introductory course in Computer Science lays the groundwork for students' future studies in the field. It offers a broad overview of essential topics, helping students to understand how computer systems operate. Key subjects include computer architecture, operating systems, networks, software development, data organization and visualization, as well as the potential applications of computing technology.
